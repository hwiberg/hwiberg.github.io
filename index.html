Biography

Hello! I am a third year PhD candidate at the Operations Research Center at MIT, working with Professor Dimitris Bertsimas. My research leverages optimization and machine learning techniques in healthcare. I am working to develop novel approaches to improve patient care based on individual characteristics, including medical history and genomic data. I am particularly interested in the interpretability of machine learning models and applications to oncology. I previously worked at athenahealth, a healthcare IT company, on their Data Science team.
